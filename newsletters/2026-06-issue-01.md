**News from the cooperative — June 2026**

Hi friends,

It's been a beautiful stretch at RegenHub. The energy has been *popping* — every
happy hour somehow more alive than the last — and since Boulder Startup Week we've
welcomed a wave of new folks coming to co-work and find community with us. If you've
felt the pull to come spend a day here, consider this your invitation.

### Going deep on Techne

After a month-long series exploring **Technology and the Human Future**, we've been
sitting with the idea of *Techne* — and what it means for how we build as a
cooperative. It's becoming a throughline for the kind of place we want RegenHub to
be: technology in service of being more human, not less.

That exploration has been getting richer through our **weekly guest dialogues**:

- Two weeks ago, **Griff Green** joined us on Ethereum, funding public goods, and his
  work reviving "The DAO."
- Last week, **Nathan Schneider** facilitated a discussion on **Magnifica Humanitas**
  — Pope Leo XIV's new encyclical on AI — and what it can teach us about the work
  we're doing here.
- **This week**, **Austin Wade Smith** joins us for *Building with Broad Data* — a walk
  through his River Computer prototypes and how real-world context can ground distributed
  intelligence, value, and new institutional experiments. He frames it through "broad
  data": scale through distributed plurality, not centralized volume.

### AI Nights, (almost) every Thursday

We've started hosting **AI Nights** most Thursdays, with rotating themes —
local/sovereign AI, vibecode nights, agent building, and more. For now these are open
to the public; we're also beginning to explore **member-only gatherings** so we can go
deeper with our most committed community.

### A more accessible way to join

We recently introduced **tiered memberships**, so you can become a member even if a
full-time co-working spot isn't your thing. It starts at **$30/month**, which includes
one free co-working day a month plus access to member-only channels and events. We
want joining the hub to be within reach for more people.

If you're curious about going deeper with us, the best next step is simply to reach out —
start a conversation with any of us you know, or come
[grab a free day pass](https://regenhub.xyz) and meet the community in person.

### Building the cooperative

Behind the scenes, we've been hard at work building RegenHub as a **Limited
Cooperative Association**. We fully incorporated on **February 6th** this year, and
have been fleshing out bylaws and member agreements ever since. Our focus right now:
strengthening our member systems, bringing more people in to co-work, and growing into
a genuine gathering place and Schelling point for community in Boulder.

We'd love for you to deepen with us — as community, and as co-workers.
