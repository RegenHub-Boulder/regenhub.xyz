Hi friends,

It's been a good couple of weeks at the hub. Lots of music, a fair bit of AI tinkering, and more than a few bikes rolling out into the evening. Here's some of what's been going on.

## Vibe Night (formerly AI Nights)

We renamed our weekly AI night. It's **Vibe Night** now, which honestly just fits better. Same idea as before: show up, mess around with whatever's interesting in AI, learn from each other. Last week we got into local and sovereign computing.

This **Thursday at 5pm** we're switching gears to **AV and creativity**: Suno for music, Palmier for video, and whatever else people feel like bringing. Bring your bike too, because we head straight out to the **Cruiser Ride** at 7:15 after.

## Friday dialogue

At our Friday dialogue we got to host **[Austin Wade Smith](https://www.austinwadesmith.com/)**. He walked us through **[River Computer](https://rivercomputer.com/)**, his work on coordination between people, living systems, and machines, plus a project called **[Waka](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/austin-wade-smith-65544932_from-my-experience-working-at-the-intersection-ugcPost-7446751698859212800-p86Y/)**. Thanks Austin — that one stuck with a lot of us.

## Our resident ventures, woven in

Part of what makes this place different: you're not just using tools here, you get a hand in shaping the ones the whole cooperative runs on.

**Gitcoin** has been deep in decentralized tech lately, **ATProto** especially, and we're preparing to build some of it into how we organize events in Boulder — so the way we coordinate becomes something the community can own and help govern, not just rent from a platform.

**Parachute** just shared a reflection on its first year building personal, self-hostable AI (**[The mind is like a parachute](https://parachute.computer/blog/the-mind-is-like-a-parachute/)**), and we've started using it to organize some of the cooperative's internal knowledge. They're opening the beta to more community folks too, so if owning your own AI and knowledge is your thing, come say hi.

It feels good when the tools we use are ones we get to help build and steer.

## The AV Club keeps growing

Our AV Club steward has been quietly building out the setup, digital and analog both. Newest addition: a **vibro-acoustic bed**, on top of the growing pile of drums. At the last Happy Hour someone kicked off a drum and instrument circle that drifted right into our gratitude circle, and honestly it was kind of magic. Come find out.
